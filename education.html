Executive Education

Academic teaching and professional development programmes

Board Governance Workshop

University of Johannesburg – Board Governance Programme

Since 2009, Professor Esser has been involved in the Board Governance Workshop at the University of Johannesburg. This programme is accredited by the Institute of Directors South Africa (IoDSA) and equips directors and senior executives with the governance knowledge and skills required for effective board participation.

IoDSA Accredited Since 2009 University of Johannesburg

ESG Investing Training

ESG Investing

Specialist training programme on ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) investing, co-delivered with Professor Piet Delport. This programme covers the regulatory landscape, practical integration of ESG factors into investment decisions, and the evolving role of corporate purpose in sustainable finance.

ESG Sustainable Finance With Prof Piet Delport

University of Glasgow Teaching

Professor Esser teaches across the undergraduate and postgraduate programmes at the University of Glasgow School of Law, with a focus on:

Corporate Governance

Theoretical and practical perspectives on governance frameworks, board structures, and shareholder engagement.

Corporate Social Responsibility

The legal and regulatory dimensions of CSR, stakeholder theory, and the evolving expectations of corporations.

Company Law

Core company law principles including directors' duties, corporate personality, and shareholder rights.

Postgraduate Supervision

Supervision of PhD and LLM research students in the UK and South Africa on topics in corporate governance and law.
